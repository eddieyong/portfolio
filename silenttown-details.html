The Silent Town - Survival Horror Game

A chilling survival horror game where players take on the role of Jack Carter, a stranded traveler searching for a car key to escape while evading the vengeful Crooked Maiden.

Key features:

  • Dynamic AI system with unpredictable enemy stalking mechanics
  • Atmospheric horror environment with detailed forest and house settings
  • Interactive puzzle elements for story progression
  • Advanced lighting and sound design for immersive experience
  • Custom-built blueprints for gameplay mechanics

Contribution:

A fresh take on survival horror, blending tension, strategy, and storytelling for an unforgettable experience. The game features sophisticated AI behavior patterns, atmospheric level design, and engaging puzzle mechanics.
